About Us

Vibra Coal Power Corporation

Vibra Coal Power Corporation (VCPC) is a duly accredited coal trader by the Department of Energy and a registered private corporation headquartered in Greenhills, San Juan City.

Established in 2020, VIBRA operates with a coal yard in Brgy. Puting Bato, Calaca, Batangas to support demand in South Luzon and reduce logistics limitations for partner clients.

Mission

Better fuel options for industrial operations.

To provide better fuel options for industrial coal boiler operators and end-users that will later be translated to huge savings for our partner clients.

Vision

Best value, products, and support.


To be able to provide the best products and services to our partner clients and give them the best value for their money.
To provide high-quality technical support and promote efficient coal-to-steam output ratio.

Product Line

Local and imported coal supply.

VIBRA supplies coal options for Philippine manufacturing industries, boiler operators, and industrial end-users.

Local CoalSemirara Coal

Locally sourced steam coal

Our local coal is sourced from Semirara Coal Corporation in Semirara Island, Antique. Local sourcing helps support supply availability within the country.

  • Calorific value: 5400 to 5800 Kcal/kg ADB
  • Ash content: 5-9%
  • Moisture content: 20-30%
Imported CoalIndonesian Coal

Imported coal for stable supply

VIBRA imports coal from Indonesian coal mines to provide stable supply at a competitive price for industrial requirements.

  • Calorific value: 5800 to 6300 Kcal/kg ADB
  • Ash content: 3-7%
  • Moisture content: 25-30%
Coal Sieving Facility

Available coal sizes.

VIBRA provides coal sieving and sizing services to match boiler requirements and customer demand.

Grate Size (0-2")

Recommended for users with chain grate boilers.

Picked Size (2-4")

Recommended for users with coal crushers or fixed grate boilers.

Special Size (0-1")

Recommended for fluidized bed boilers and step grate boilers.
